What is Flexa Tech Mode

Flexa Bot Tech Mode offer introduces several groundbreaking features that are not available on the free Flexa plan.
The Tech Mode enhances the user experience by offering advanced functionalities designed to streamline and optimize various aspects
of the Flexa Bot service.

What does it include

  1. Customization Options: Premium users gain access to a wide range of customization options, allowing them to tailor the Flexa Bot experience to their specific needs and preferences.

  2. Priority Support: Premium subscribers receive priority customer support, ensuring faster response times and dedicated assistance from the Flexa support team.

  3. Advanced Analytics: The premium version of Flexa Bot offers advanced analytics features, providing users with deeper insights into their transaction data and spending patterns.

  4. Exclusive Features: Premium users also gain access to exclusive features and tools not available on the free Flexa plan.

If you ever need support, please contact support by the widget in the bottom right. Or via the email: support@flexagroup.co.uk.
