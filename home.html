Our Apologies

On 13th December 2024, The Flexa Discord Bot was breached. The hacker(s) used Flexa to execute a malicious command in every server the bot is present in, that removes all the server's channels and chats, as well as resetting or granting everyone all roles.

We are deeply apologetic for this event and we are currently looking into how it occurred and to upgrade our security to make sure it does not happen again.

In the meantime, please refrain from creating any new channels or roles in any servers you have access to until further notice, as the hacker(s) may still have the server information. We recommend you create a new server and DO NOT promote it in the old one.

Also refrain from attempting to purchase any Flexa products, including "Tech Mode" (Premium), until further notice.

Again, we are deeply sorry for this event and we guarantee it will not happen again in the future.
-The Flexa Team.
